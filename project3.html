Book Cover

Fall 2022

About This Project

This project is from my graphic design class in the fall semester of 2022. I was given the assignment to design your own book cover for a book of your choice. I chose Alice's Adventures In Wonderland by Lewis Carroll and I focused on the Red Queen's garden for the book cover design. I took photos of all of the props. I used a sheet of moss as the background and a piece of glass to tape the playing cards on and to create a shadow to make the illusion of them falling. I painted the artificial white roses with red paint as a reference to a popular scene in the book. I used Adobe Photoshop to enhance the photos I took and add the necessecary text and publication images a book cover includes. I also used the application to make a 3D mockup of the design.
