The Mysterious Myths And Legends of Latin America

April-May 2023

About This Project

This project is from my digital illustration class in the spring semester of 2023. I was given the assignment to create a zine comic with the topic of my choice. Along with preknown knowledge, I researched additional information of the Latin American myths and legends I chose for this project. I drew the illustrations on paper first and scanned them digitally to be able to add details and color on Adobe Illustrator. I also used Adobe Illustrator to add typography, images, and additional illustrations/graphics.
