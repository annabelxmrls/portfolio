The Mysterious Myths And Legends of Latin America

April-May 2023

About This Project

This project is from my digital interface design class in the spring semester of 2023. I was given the assignment to pitch an app idea with the purpose of helping a community. I chose to create an app that will benefit the health of cats and dogs by introducing their owners to nutritious recipes. This project includes a pitch deck, prototype, and support website that was all created in Figma.
